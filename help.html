Giving Elders A Life Of Dignity In Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh

Welcome to SERUDS Happy Old Age Home, a sanctuary for abandoned and destitute women in Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh. Our home provides a safe and nurturing environment where elders can live with dignity and respect during the last stage of their lives.


Since our launch in 2012, we have provided free shelter to 90 elders, and currently, 30 grannies call our home their own. Our Old Age Home is fully funded through donations from individuals and corporates who share our vision of giving back to society.
